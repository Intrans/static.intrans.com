Intrans uses location data from users’ mobile phone to helps those users match available truckload capacity with loads from shippers nearby. When you haul a load for a shipper who uses Intrans, we will provide the shipper with periodic updates on your location. You can opt-out at any time by sending STOP to 650-409-3131.

Terms & Conditions: Mobile phone user privacy is extremely important to us at Intrans. Location coordinates are only gathered after the mobile phone user has agreed to use this service. The agreement process is commenced by a shipper or a member of Intrans’s team sending an Opt-In SMS text message or phone call to the user’s mobile phone. The user can then accept this agreement by replying YES to the Opt-In SMS text message or by pressing the number '1' on the automated phone call. At any time the user can cancel Intrans’s ability to obtain their location coordinates by replying STOP to this Opt-In SMS text message.

Location coordinates (longitude, latitude, and radius) will be obtained only when we have proper authorization from the user.

Location coordinates (longitude, latitude) from mobile phones will be stored for up to 30 days.

Message and Data rates may apply. Messages will be sent to your phone periodically to alert you to loads available for pickup near your current location or to alert you to changes in a load’s status.

Opt-In/Opt-Out

  • Our system will send a text or phone call from 650-409-3131, reply 'YES' to the text or press '1' on on the phone call to opt-in.
  • To cancel: Text STOP to 650-409-3131.
  • For Support, text HELP to 650-409-3131 or email help@intrans.com. At any time, users may cancel Intrans’s ability to obtain their location coordinates by going to www.intrans.com/optout.

Privacy – All customer data will be used only by Intrans and our partners to provide these services. We will not pass-on or re-sell your data to any third party. Location coordinate data is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology into password protected servers. This information is securely retained in memory long enough for Intrans to determine and send appropriate load opportunities.

Your Consent – By using Intrans, the user consents to our Privacy Policy. To review our Privacy Policy please visit www.intrans.com/privacy.

Limitations – Intrans’ location service is only available on AT&T, Verizon, Sprint, and T-Mobile.

Changes to Privacy Policy – In the case of changes to Intrans’s Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on www.intrans.com/privacy.
